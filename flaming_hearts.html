Flaming Hearts


Oh, how I remember!
All those years 'til now.
Passion glowed, an ember.
We cared not why nor how.

At first, I wore my shell.
Then it broke beyond repair.
In its place you dwell,
A face for all my care.

People they come and they go.
But not their need to despise.
Our journey it's eager in tow.
We go where the flame never dies.
